Cold Press Machine

High-Performance Cold Press Solutions for Woodworking Industries
Makewell Industries is a leading manufacturer of woodworking cold pressing machines, offering a variety of options such as 50 Ton Hydraulic Cold Press Machines, 100 Ton Cold Press Machines, and more. Our machines are designed for industrial use, providing robust performance and efficient operation in a variety of woodworking applications. Based in Ahmedabad, India, Makewell Industries has been serving the industry since 1993, delivering high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective solutions to clients worldwide.
Our Cold Press Machines are known for their superior build quality, designed with hydraulic power sources for smooth, precise, and uniform pressure distribution. With options for both manual and automatic operation, these machines cater to a wide range of industrial requirements, ensuring maximum productivity and performance.
